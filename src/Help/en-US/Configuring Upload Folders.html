Configuring Upload Folders

Individual upload folders can be configured by adding, editing, and deleting; each having its own PDF Information Properties. These properties will be applied to each PDF document during the upload process.

  • From the PDFKeeper main form, select the Tools menu, and then select Manage Upload Folder Configurations; or select the Manage Upload Folder Configurations tool bar icon; or press Alt + T and then U on the keyboard. Once the Manage Upload Folder Configurations form opens, you can Add, Edit, and Delete Upload Folder Configurations.
