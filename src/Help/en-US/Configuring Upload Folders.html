Configuring Upload Folders

Individual upload folders can be configured by adding, editing, and deleting; each having its own PDF Information Properties; these properties will be applied to each PDF document during the upload process.

  • From the PDFKeeper main form, either select the Tools menu, and then select Upload Folders; or select the Upload Folders tool bar icon; or press Alt + T and then U on the keyboard. Once the Upload Folders form opens, you can Add, Edit, and Delete Configured Folders.
