Uploading PDF Documents from outside of PDFKeeper

  • Using Windows Explorer, copy the PDF document(s) that you wish to upload and paste into PDFKeeper Upload, located in the Windows Documents folder.

Additional Information:

  • PDFKeeper must be open for the PDFKeeper Upload folder shortcut to be available in the Windows Documents folder.

  • The PDF document(s) must meet the criteria specified in the Uploading PDF Documents topic; otherwise, any that don't will be rejected by the Upload process.
