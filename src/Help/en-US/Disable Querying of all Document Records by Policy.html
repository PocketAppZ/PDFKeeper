Disable Querying of all Document Records by Policy

In a multi user environment, with a database that contains thousands of documents, it may be required to limit who can use the query all document records feature.

  • To disable the feature, apply DisableQueryAllDocuments.reg (included with PDFKeeper) to the targeted computers. Elevated privileges are required to apply this change.

  • To re-enable the feature, apply EnableQueryAllDocuments.reg (included with PDFKeeper) to the targeted computers. Elevated privileges are required to apply this change.
